# CoffeeChain Management

## Project Title

CoffeeChain Management

## Project Description

CoffeeChain Management is a decentralized coffee shop management platform built using Soroban smart contracts on the Stellar blockchain. The system enables coffee shop owners to manage menu items, customer orders, inventory, and sales records in a transparent, secure, and trustless manner.

By storing operational data on-chain, CoffeeChain Management ensures data integrity, auditability, and protection against unauthorized modifications while simplifying day-to-day business operations.

## Project Vision

The vision of CoffeeChain Management is to modernize coffee shop operations through decentralized technology. By leveraging blockchain infrastructure, the platform aims to provide business owners with transparent record keeping, secure data storage, and reliable business management without dependence on centralized systems.

## Key Features

* **Menu Management:** Create, update, and remove coffee shop products and menu items.
* **Order Management:** Record and manage customer orders directly on-chain.
* **Inventory Tracking:** Monitor stock quantities and product availability.
* **Sales Recording:** Store transaction history for future auditing and analysis.
* **Revenue Monitoring:** Track business income and sales performance.
* **Access Control:** Restrict administrative operations to authorized owners.
* **Immutable Records:** All critical business data is securely stored on the Stellar blockchain.
* **Transparent Queries:** Anyone can verify menu information and order records as permitted by the contract.

## Usage Instructions

1. **Deploy Contract:** Deploy the smart contract to the Stellar network.
2. **Set Owner:** Assign the coffee shop owner as the contract administrator.
3. **Add Products:** Create menu items with names, prices, and stock quantities.
4. **Manage Inventory:** Update stock levels whenever products are added or sold.
5. **Create Orders:** Record customer purchases through smart contract functions.
6. **Track Revenue:** Query sales and revenue information from the blockchain.
7. **Update Menu:** Modify product information whenever necessary.

## Future Scope

* **Token-Based Payments:** Accept Stellar assets and Soroban tokens for payments.
* **Customer Loyalty Program:** Implement reward points and membership benefits.
* **NFT Membership Cards:** Issue NFT-based customer memberships.
* **Multi-Branch Support:** Manage multiple coffee shop locations from a single contract.
* **Analytics Dashboard:** Provide real-time sales and inventory analytics.
* **Mobile Application Integration:** Connect the smart contract to mobile ordering systems.
* **Supplier Management:** Track suppliers and inventory restocking activities.

## Technology Stack

* Rust and Soroban SDK for smart contract development.
* Stellar blockchain for decentralized and immutable data storage.
* Smart contract authentication and authorization using Stellar accounts.
* On-chain storage for menu, inventory, and order management.
